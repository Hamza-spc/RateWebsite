Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a venue?

Only administrators can add venues. If you're an admin, log in and go to the Admin Dashboard to add new venues.

How do I rate a venue?

You need to be logged in to rate venues. Visit any venue's detail page and click "Rate & Review" to share your experience.

Can I edit my review?

Yes, you can edit your review by visiting the venue's page and clicking on your existing rating.
